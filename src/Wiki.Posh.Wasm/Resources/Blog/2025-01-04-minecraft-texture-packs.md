---
title: Minecraft Texture Packs 
---

<p>
    Just a quick collection of the resource packs I use in Minecraft!
    This is for Java edition, as I don't play much Bedrock these days.
</p>
<p>
    You won't need any fancy tools to install these, just drop them into
    your packs folder. They're all compatible with 1.21.3, despite what Minecraft
    and CurseForge might tell you.
</p>
<p>
    The load order is important: From top to bottom, they should be loaded in
    the order in which they appear on this page.
</p>

<p>

        href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/shaders/astralex-shader-bsl-edit">AstraLex</a>,

<h3>
    <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/rainbow-xp-bar-and-ping">
        Rainbow XP bar
    </a>
</h3>
<p>

<h3>
    <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/colourful-containers-dark-mode-gui-optifine">

</h3>

<h3>
    <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/fancy-gui-overhaul">
        Fancy GUI overhaul
    </a>
</h3>
<p>
    Similar to the colourful containers pack, this pack overhauls the UI.
    I prefer colourful containers' block UIs, but Fancy also applies some edits to the HUD.
</p>

    <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/techys-bushy-leaves">
        Techy's Bushy Leaves

<h3>
    <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/vanilla-exp">
        Vanilla Experience+
    </a>
</h3>
<p>
    My favourite thing about this experience pack is how it changes the texture
    of spawn eggs to more clearly reflect the mobs they're associated with.
    It also adds better flowers, animated ender chests, sticky piston sides,
    and a whole lot more minor quality-of-life texture changes.
</p>
<figure class="figure">
    <img class="figure-img img-fluid rounded" style="max-height: 20em;"
        src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/949/355/items.webp">
</figure>

<h3>
    <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/nature-x">
        Nature X
    </a>
</h3>
<p>
    Some great modifications to nature-related blocks. Plants feel a lot less blocky
    and a lot more bushy, and biomes feel much less clean, far more alive.

<h3>
    <a href="https://www.planetminecraft.com/texture-pack/custom-3d-minecart/">
        Medieval Minecart
    </a>
</h3>
<p>

    less like an iron box and more like a fit-for-purpose mining vehicle. Fair warning,

    download it directly<a href="https://www.mediafire.com/file/oxwbf19f9o185gs/Medieval+Minecart.zip/file">!</a>
